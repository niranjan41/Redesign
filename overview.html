*Categorical*: Nominal, Ordinal - *Continuous*: Temporal, Quantiative **Transformations** - Tall <-> Wide - Aggregation (Bins) - Basic Stats (Min, Max, Average) - New Variables - Geocoding - Filtering, Refining --- # **Visual Layer** - *Marks*: Points, Tick, Lines, Bar, Area, Glyphs, Polygon, ... - *Channels*: Position, Size, Color, Shape, Path, ... - *Position*: Identity, Stacked, Jitter, Dodge - *Scale*: Linear, Log, Nominal, Color, Size - *Coordinate*: Cartesian, Polar, Geographic, Parallel - *Layout*: Single, Faceting, Layer, Repeat, Concat --- # **Annotation Layer** - *Title* & *Labels* - *Axis* and *Tick marks* - *Legends* - *Grids* and *Reference* - *Text Annotation* - *Story Elements* --- # **Interaction Layer** - *Selection*: Discrete (single, multi), Continuous (interval), Voronoi (nearest), Remove (toggle) - *Events*: `click`, `dblclick`, `mouseover`, `drag`,... - *Condition*: Change encoding, scale, filter, ... - *Bind*: Input elements (range, radio-box,...) - *Project*: default, fields - *Translate*: scale --- # **Visual Vocabulary** - *Deviation* - *Correlation* - *Ranking* - *Distribution* - *Magnitude* - *Part-to-Whole* - *Change over time* - *Spatial* - *Flow* --- # **Interaction Patterns** - **Select**: mark something as interesting - **Explore**: show me something else - **Encode**: show me a different representation - **Abstract/Elaborate**: show me more or less detail - **Filter**: show me something conditionally - **Connect**: show me related items - **Reconfigure**: show me a different arrangement - **Transition**: move between different views --- # **Interaction Vocabulary** - Select e.g. *Highlighting* - Explore e.g. *Navigation like Pan, Zoom* - Encode e.g. *Change Color, Position* - Abstract/Elaborate e.g. *Overview & Details* - Filter e.g. *Dynamic Queries*, *Cross Filter* - Connect e.g. *Brushing & Linking* - Reconfigure e.g. *Sorting*, *Indexing* - Transition e.g. *Staging & Animation* --- # Visualisation Mantra > "Overview first, zoom and filter, then details-on-demand." > -- Ben Shneiderman Reference: [The Surest Path to Visual Discovery by Stephen Few](http://www.b-eye-network.com/view/2674) --- # **Data Wrangling** -
